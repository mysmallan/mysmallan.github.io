Slottsfjellsfestivalen: Festival poster & schedule

In my edition of poster and program for the Slottsfjell Festival, I have focused on creating a strong visual identity that appeals to the target audience. I think that hand-drawn and humorous images are popular among young people today and catch their attention. The idea behind the identity is to use different animals with different clothes based on music genres. As you can see, I have a "Punk-Pig" on the poster and a "Hippie-Dog" on the program, so this can be further extended and you can for example have a "Glam Rock-Horse" on the website or a "Pop-Cow" in a brochure. The idea is to showcase the typical festival character in a funny setting.
