Poppis: a redesign of Maarud micropopcorn

We realised that Maarud micropopcorn is almost twice as expensive as the competitors but still looks exactly the same. We therefore choosed to redesign the package in order to make it look more attractive. The work consisted of several strategic phases.

our problem to solve

"How can you make a relation between price and presentation, and make the product stand out from the crowd?"
