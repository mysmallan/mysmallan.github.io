Börtnans Fjällvattenfisk

Börtnans Fjällvattenfisk is a company that produce and sell fish products of various kinds. They needed a new website where they could present their products and tell their story. The company wanted to communicate its three values: quality, craftsmanship and the fact that it's locally produced.

I had the opportunity to take responsibility for the whole process, including photography, design and coding HTML and CSS in collaboration with a web developer.
